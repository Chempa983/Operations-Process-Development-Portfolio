PESISTTEMA — Public Emergency Safety Information System Through Technology

Strategic concept • System design • Public safety operations

Concept Summary

I designed PESISTTEMA as a unified municipal safety platform that connects citizens, municipalities, and emergency stakeholders through real-time reporting, verified identity, and structured operational workflows.

Core Modules

  • Citizen incident reporting (geo + media evidence)
  • Location-based alerts (severity-based)
  • SOS function with automatic location sharing
  • Operations dashboard for prioritization & follow-up
  • Analytics layer for trend detection and resource planning

Operational Value

  • Faster signal-to-response through standardized intake
  • Higher reporting trust through verified identity options
  • Transparent communication workflows (who does what, when)
  • Scalable architecture for multi-city expansion
Positioning

This is a concept-development project demonstrating system thinking, risk logic, and operational execution design for public-sector scale.
